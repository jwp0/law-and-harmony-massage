Meet Your Therapist

Sandy Lazzaro

Owner & Licensed Massage Therapist

As the owner of Law and Harmony Massage, Sandy is dedicated to providing exceptional therapeutic services that promote wellness and relaxation. Her passion for massage therapy drives her to create a tranquil environment where clients can rejuvenate both body and mind.

Sandy continuously pursues professional development to enhance her skills and stay updated on the latest techniques. Her goal is to foster a harmonious experience for every individual who walks through her doors — and to build lasting relationships that support each client's journey toward better health.
